CS395 - Ethics of Artificial Intelligence

Welcome to our website,

The goal of our project is to discuss ethical concerns surrounding artificial intelligence with regards to its militaristic characteristics. We aim to analyze the morality, frameworks, challenges, policy vacuums, and physical limitations, that are being produced from the development of such technologies. With in-depth detail and evidence, we will convey our standpoint on this topic.

The top navigation bar details primary viewpoints, with our overall product being "The Video." In our video, we propose the aforementioned concerns and come to a conclusion after all issues have been considered and discussed. On the left navigation bar, we present our summaries for each subtopic of the overall product.
